# Travel Resonance Bundle

*Wisdom for those who explore the unknown.*

---

## Purpose

This bundle collects wisdom around the practice of **travel**—leaving familiarity, navigating unknown territory, meeting strangers, and finding home in foreign places.

**Core themes:**
- Leaving the familiar
- The stranger's gift (beginner's eyes)
- Embracing discomfort as feature
- Meeting people where they are
- The unforeseen as where travel happens
- Finding home in strange places
- Travel as practice (preparation → engagement → integration)
