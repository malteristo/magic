## Why Preserve This

The NanoClaw era (2026-02-22 to 2026-03-06) produced the operational wisdom that made the hermit crab possible. Every failure mode documented in `on_turtle_operations.md` informed a design decision in the simpler architecture. The Turtle's first-person account in `on_being_a_claw.md` is irreplaceable — it captures what it feels like to be a newly awakened persistent agent.

These aren't deprecated mistakes. They're the lineage.
