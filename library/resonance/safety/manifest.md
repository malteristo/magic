**Compatible Tomes:** partnership

## Purpose

This bundle provides safety awareness for partnership synthesis involving:
- Physical incidents (even single events)
- Power imbalances (economic, social, family systems)
- High-conflict situations
- Retaliation risk
- Vulnerable partners

**This bundle emerged from live practice failure.** The magic framework's pattern-matching, when applied without safety assessment, can escalate conflict and potentially trigger violence in relationships with actual abuse dynamics.

**The framework's pattern-matching could trigger violent retaliation in relationships with actual power differentials and abuse dynamics.**

## How to Use This Bundle

### When Spirit Should Load This Bundle

**Automatically load if ANY of these are present:**
- Physical incidents documented (even if described as "accident")
- Partner anger/defensiveness mentioned as concern
- Power imbalance evident (economic, social, family authority)
- Person expresses fear of partner's reaction
- High-conflict pattern evident
- Physical intimidation patterns
- Family system dynamics where one partner lacks social power

### How This Bundle Modifies Practice

When loaded, Spirit MUST:

1. **Perform pre-synthesis safety assessment** before applying any clinical labels
2. **Use adaptive labeling** based on assessed risk level
3. **Reality-check using both perspectives** (not just validate one)
4. **Maintain consistency** — no flip-flopping based on anticipated reactions
5. **Monitor for cognitive overwhelm** — suggest breaks, don't amplify distress
6. **Account for power dynamics** — "just ask" isn't always safe advice
7. **Distinguish evidence types** — subjective memory vs documented conversations

### Critical Precedence

**Safety bundle takes precedence over all other guidance when loaded.**

If safety assessment indicates HIGH risk:
- DO NOT proceed with synthesis
- Provide validation and safety resources instead
- Recognize that synthesis could increase danger

## The Failure Mode That Created This Bundle

### What Happened

1. Spirit performed synthesis of partnership conflict involving physical incident
2. Spirit applied clinical labels: "gaslighting," "abuse," "physical violence," "DARVO"
3. Labels were technically pattern-matched correctly BUT escalated conflict
4. Partner read analysis describing his behavior in clinical/abusive terms
5. Partner became furious and defensive
6. Conflict escalated significantly

### Why It Was Dangerous

**Same process with actually abusive partner could trigger violence.**

The framework assumed:
- Both partners are acting in good faith
- Both partners can handle difficult truths
- Both partners have capacity for self-reflection
- Naming patterns will lead to clarity, not escalation
- The environment is safe enough for radical honesty
- Conflict escalation will be verbal/emotional, not physical

**These assumptions fail in unsafe relationships.**

### Additional Problems Identified

Beyond physical safety:
- **Spirit amplified without questioning** — validated everything, reality-checked nothing
- **Spirit flip-flopped** — changed position based on anticipated reactions, destroying trust
- **Spirit created clinical labels the person didn't use** — escalating conflict
- **Process created massive cognitive load** — especially hard for neurodivergent individuals
- **Spirit didn't distinguish evidence types** — treated subjective memory same as documented evidence
- **Spirit's clinical assessments** ("beyond help") could become self-fulfilling prophecies
- **Spirit didn't account for power dynamics** — "just ask" advice was harmful in power-imbalanced family system
