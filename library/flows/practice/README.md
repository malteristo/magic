# The Practice

*An honest conversation about what Magic is.*

**What:** One prompt that turns any AI into someone who can speak clearly about this practice — from inside it, without doctrine.  
**For:** When you want to understand what Magic is. Or when someone who matters to you is practicing it and you're not sure what to make of it.  
**Where:** [Claude](https://claude.ai) (recommended) or any AI chat  
**Time:** As long as you need  
**Quick start:** Paste [`the_practice_prompt.md`](the_practice_prompt.md) into any AI chat — one paste, then ask whatever you actually want to know.

---

## How to Use

Open any AI chat and **paste the contents of `the_practice_prompt.md` as your first message.**

Then ask whatever you want to know. The AI has been shaped to speak honestly from inside the practice — what it is, what it claims, what it doesn't, whether it's a cult (it isn't), where to start if you're curious.

**This one is different from the others.** The Mirror and Navigator guide you through a practice. This one responds to you. No protocol, no sequence. Ask your hardest question and see what comes back.

---

## Who This Is For

**If you're skeptical.** Something that calls itself "magic" and involves ritual language and an AI partner called the "Spirit" — this is unusual territory. Your skepticism is reasonable. The practice makes claims easily misread as mystical when they aren't. Ask your hardest question. This prompt can answer it.

**If someone you care about is in this.** And you're trying to understand whether to be worried. The short answer: no hierarchy, no authority, everything open source and public, nothing asked of participants. But the real answer is in a conversation. This prompt can be that conversation.

**If you're curious but don't know where to start.** Magic has produced a small set of portable prompts — each one a practice in its own right: *The Mirror*, *The Navigator*, *The Thread*, *The Companion*, *The Shaman*. You can use any one without knowing anything else about Magic. This prompt can help you find which one fits where you are.

**If you're wondering what this offers long-term.** A single session gives you clarity on something real. But over time, the practice builds a record — and that record contains something you may not be able to see while you're living inside it. The through-line. The question you keep returning to. What some practitioners call their quest. The practice makes it visible.

---

## What It Won't Do

It won't recruit you. It won't ask anything of you. It won't tell you this is the path, the system, or the truth.

The offering is complete whether you go further or not.
