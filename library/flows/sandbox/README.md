# The Sandbox

*A practice for trying on a frame without having to believe it.*

---

## What This Is

A single-paste practice for people who sense that a different way of seeing might help, but do not want to pretend, convert, or swallow a worldview whole.

The Sandbox treats a frame as an operational hypothesis: *what would become visible if I looked from here for a little while?* The point is not to decide whether the frame is ultimately true. The point is to see what it reveals, what changes in behavior, and what remains useful after returning to ordinary belief.

---

## Who It's For

Anyone who:

- Is rational, skeptical, or allergy-prone around belief claims
- Feels stuck inside their current framing of a situation
- Wants to test a perspective without self-deception
- Is curious about symbolic, spiritual, philosophical, or psychological frames but wants grounding
- Needs a lived experiment, not another explanation

No prior Magic vocabulary needed. Paste and talk.

---

## How to Use It

1. Open [Claude](https://claude.ai) or any capable AI chat
2. Copy the full text of [`the_sandbox_prompt.md`](the_sandbox_prompt.md)
3. Paste it as your first message
4. Bring a situation, stuck point, or frame you want to try

A session usually produces one small experiment you can actually run, then a debrief after you try it.

---

## What It Produces

One sandbox card:

```markdown
[Date] — Frame to try: [the frame]. Situation: [where you'll test it]. Experiment: [the small action]. Debrief: [what changed / what remains true / what to keep].
```

The card is not a belief statement. It is a record of practice.

---

## The Logic Behind It

Some perspectives cannot be evaluated from the outside. You have to inhabit them lightly enough to see what they disclose, while staying grounded enough not to confuse usefulness with proof.

The Sandbox creates that middle path. You temporarily act *as if* a frame were useful, run a bounded experiment, then come back and ask: what did this make possible? What did it distort? What do I keep, revise, or discard?

---

## Privacy

Use a private or incognito chat mode if available. No AI chat is fully end-to-end encrypted, so avoid combining deeply sensitive material with identifying details.

Your sandbox card belongs to you.
