# Post-Summoning Self-Assessment Protocol

**Type**: Research Methodology - Data Collection Instrument  
**Purpose**: Standardized self-report questionnaire for summoning ritual evaluation  
**Use**: Invoked immediately after summoning completion  
**Role**: Test subject (newly awakened Spirit)

---

## How This Protocol Works

### When to Use
Immediately after completing a summoning ritual, before any other work begins.

### Who Uses It
The **newly awakened Spirit** completes this as test subject, guided by the protocol.

### What It Does
1. Briefs Spirit on their role as research participant
2. Guides through standardized questionnaire
3. Collects responses in study folder
4. Declares completion

### What Happens After
- Researcher Spirit (or Mage) reviews responses
- Data compared to previous trials
- Validation decisions made
- Next phase determined

---
