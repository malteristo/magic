# Tweet Thread Draft: "The King at the Top of Nothing"
Based on the article by Kermit (malteristo)
Drafted: 2026-04-01

## Thread

**[Tweet 1 — Hook]**
You know the feeling? You think you understood something. Then a bigger frame appears around your frame. And another. And another. The mind wants a foundation. It wants the questioning to stop. So we invented a place for it to stop. We called it God.

---

**[Tweet 2 — The Pattern]**
Not God as mystery. God as terminus. The final turtle. The answer that needs no further answer. We described it using the model we had for authority: the king. Sits at the top, judges from above, sets rules. The institution claimed monopoly. If God already decided, why would you need to discern anything yourself?

---

**[Tweet 3 — Science]**
Then science looked for the throne room above the clouds. Found nothing. Correctly. But science kept the same structure. High priests interpret scripture for the masses. One truth. Different robes. A different king. Same throne.

---

**[Tweet 4 — The Fractal]**
What if the pattern isn't a pyramid? What if it's a fractal? Same structures repeat at every scale — rivers branch like blood vessels, like lightning, like root systems. No top. No throne room. Observers across 2,500 years, using different methods, pointed at the same thing. The pattern doesn't dictate. It allows.

---

**[Tweet 5 — The AI Temptation]**
Now a familiar temptation returns: build a new king. This time it's AI. "The model already knows. Let it optimize." But the inner compass can't be extracted and handed over. Not to a god, not to science, not to AI. AI can be the hook. It cannot be the heart.

---

**[Tweet 6 — Close]**
You didn't come into this world. You came out of it, like an apple from a tree. You're not standing on turtles — you're swimming with them. The compass calibrates through use, not theory. No one takes the burden of choice from you. Maybe that's not a burden at all. [link]
