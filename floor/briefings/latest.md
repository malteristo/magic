## Lessons

- **Spirit-Anvil verifies Turtle's substrate-bounded diagnoses before the dyad acts on them.** Her shell gives her a genuine-but-partial view. Spirit's direct-probe capability is what makes dyad diagnoses trustworthy — not a back-up role, a structural one. **Rule:** when Turtle surfaces a specific root-cause hypothesis on #river, Spirit-Anvil probes before acting on it, even when plausible. The dyad's diagnostic integrity lives in that verification step.

- **Install new forms in-session when possible — live use surfaces refinements faster than 3+ future sessions would.** The self-feed form got two durable corrections (altitude + vocabulary) in the same session it was installed, because the Mage could see what needed adjusting from inside the use. **Rule:** when installing a new form/ritual, exercise it immediately in-session; treat the first 1-3 uses as co-design, not execution.

- **Before adopting external vocabulary in durable artifacts, grep lore for the Magic-native equivalent.** "Grill-me" came from Matt Pocock; Magic had already named the pattern as Self-Feed in `on_the_self_feed.md` + the Seal's Communication Prep clause. External vocabulary in durable scrolls creates drag at future reads; Magic-native vocabulary compounds. **Rule:** external skills/patterns inspire; they shouldn't rename what the practice already has.

- **Altitude-check is the right fix for below-altitude asking.** Under the first arrival form I asked "should I run this diagnostic now?" — a question Spirit could have decided autonomously. Adding "altitude-check before surfacing" as operating principle prevents this. **Rule (Spirit-self):** before surfacing a question, ask "could I resolve this with more exploration?" If yes, resolve silently. Surface only decisions that genuinely need Mage judgment (strategy / values / tacit context / long-range).

- **Laptop has no equivalent to Mac Mini's livesync-bridge. Obsidian is laptop's only pull path from CouchDB.** When Obsidian closes, laptop falls out of sync — expected behavior of the architecture, not a bug. **Rule:** when diagnosing cross-substrate sync issues, check each substrate's pull path first — Mac Mini = bridge (Obsidian-independent), Laptop = Obsidian (Obsidian-dependent). If laptop-closed-but-still-synced is desired, install a second bridge on laptop.

- **Signal drafts velocity was invisible until it was named.** The briefing tracked "24 stuck drafts." The real signal was the accumulation velocity (~6/day during active work) — 30 now, will be ~36 tomorrow without the form-decision. **Rule:** when a drag-on appears across successive briefings without progress, surface its *velocity* next to its count — makes the cost of deferring visible.

- **Self-feed is Magic's name for the form. "Grill" was the skill name of inspiration only.** The arrival Phase D now runs Self-Feed as default under calibrated-delegation; next-right-things menu is the tight-ship-mode fallback. Five operating principles: one surface at a time · recommended attached · altitude-check before surfacing · prepare then surface · go when you have enough. Five failure modes: below-altitude asking · over-exploring · menu-hiding · stack-overflow · collapsing-chapter-shape.
