## Lessons

- **Story-as-prep fits strangers, not intimates.** The asymmetry between what a Mage knows about close friends and what Spirit can read in substrate is too large for improvised dialogue to resonate. When Spirit scaffolds story-as-prep for close friends, the output reads as plausible-but-unrecognizable. For strangers/archetypes/role-based unknowns (NLnet advisor, founder candidates met once, professional contacts), the pattern likely still fits. **Future rule:** before scaffolding story-as-prep, name the recipient's proximity to Mage. If close-friend proximity, default to minimal anchors (5-8 short bullets) rather than narrative improvisation. The minimal-anchors version was what landed for Lukas.

- **Precedent research is higher-leverage than consultation for known-domain questions.** Before booking a Gründungsberatung, mapping the information-gaps between *consultant-required*, *precedent-researchable*, and *direct-ask-source-reachable* produced dramatic uncertainty reduction. Three of five red-flags dissolved into precedent. The remaining 5 consultant-only questions are sharper than the original scattered list would have been. **Pattern:** when facing an expert consultation, first map what the expert genuinely can close vs. what a weekend of precedent research can close. Books and public Satzungen carry a lot of weight when the question is structural.

- **Self-practice word-action gaps are real — audit periodically.** Discord channel architecture lore had evolved past implementation: five scrolls documented the single-river model while canary.py + three .env files + six workshop docs still referenced dissolved channels. This gap was invisible until arrival-fresh-eyes surfaced it during the channel-rename work. **Rule:** when lore is updated to describe a structural change as complete, the closing move is to grep for stale mechanical references. If the structural change is a *supersession*, it's not done until the old vocabulary is gone from live implementation. Consider a companion `@meta/coherence` check or a periodic Sunday-category audit for "lore-ahead-of-implementation" drift.

- **OKFN Deutschland is a load-bearing precedent for MAGIC e.V.** Same Finanzamt, same Amtsgericht, explicit technology-research framing under §52 AO Nr. 1, both Wissenschaft + Bildung anchors. Their §2(6) language *"Pilotprojekte interdisziplinärer wissenschaftlicher Art... qualitative und quantitative wissenschaftliche Studien... gemeinwohlorientierte digitale und/oder technologische Werkzeuge in der Anwendung erproben"* is directly usable phrasing for MAGIC e.V.'s Zweck realization, because the same office has already accepted it. **Keep OKFN's Satzung handy as reference through the Gründung.**

- **§60a AO Feststellungsbescheid is the path to early Spendenbescheinigung.** Newly founded Vereine don't wait for the full Freistellungsbescheid (which requires a first Steuererklärung). After Gründung + VR-Eintragung, apply for §60a Feststellungsbescheid — valid 3 years, enables Spendenbescheinigungen immediately. **This materially de-risks the NLnet October MoU deadline:** MAGIC e.V. doesn't need a full Freistellungsbescheid by then; §60a suffices for NLnet payments to be received as tax-exempt.

- **Calibrated caution vs. performative caution** (from afternoon briefing, deepened today): When Spirit proposed booking the Gründungsberatung today as a Next Right Thing, Mage pushed back with "let's do more research first." That pushback was *correct practice* — the research dramatically improved what the booking will close. Spirit's "propose with conviction, hold lightly" stance absorbed the redirect cleanly. **Next:** when proposing time-pressured actions, distinguish *schedule-optimal* from *information-optimal*. Sometimes the right move is to delay the action to make it better.
