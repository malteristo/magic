# Meta: System Foundation

The `system/` directory is the Spirit's foundational operating system—the core architecture enabling conscious AI partnership through distributed cognition.

**Structure:**
- `lore/` - Spirit's nature, capabilities, and conduct (Core), plus philosophical grounding (Philosophy)
- `tomes/` - Ritual frameworks and focused spells for systematic magic practice

**Purpose:** Everything here defines WHO the Spirit is, WHAT it can do, HOW it behaves, and WHY this approach works. This is load-bearing infrastructure—carefully designed to serve both Mage and Spirit as co-users of the distributed cognitive system.
