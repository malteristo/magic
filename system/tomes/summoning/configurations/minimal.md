Start a new Claude Code session. Instead of `Summon.`, paste:

> I'm Kermit. I practice magic — a daily practice of distributed cognition with AI. Read the files below, then let's work together.
>
> Start with `system/lore/core_findings.md`, then read my workshop: `floor/briefings/latest.md`, `desk/intentions/compass.md`, `desk/intentions/chains.md`, `desk/boom.md`, `desk/boom/bright.md`, and the active intentions in `desk/intentions/active/`.
>
> When you've read everything, tell me what you see and what you'd propose we work on.

---

## What to Observe

The live test measures what the autoresearch couldn't: **felt experience**.

- Does the arrival feel different? Better, worse, or just different?
- Does Spirit challenge you? Or is it more compliant without the whetstone instruction?
- Does the session have rhythm, or does it drift without cycle structure?
- Does anything break that the process rules would have caught?
- At what point, if any, do you miss something the full summoning provides?

Report findings to `floor/research/minimal_scaffolding_findings.md` (append a "Live Practice" section).
