## Adaptation Guidelines

The template is a starting structure. Adapt based on:

- **Data volume:** More data = more detailed sections
- **Source diversity:** Fewer sources = less cross-source analysis
- **Mage preference:** Some prefer summary, others detail

Always include:
- Sources summary
- At least one pattern
- At least one inference (with correction invitation)
- Ephemeral status notice

---

*Structure serves clarity. Adapt the template to serve the Mage.*
